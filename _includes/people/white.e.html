Ethan White is an Associate Professor in the Department of Biology and the Ecology Center at Utah State University. He is a Moore Investigator in Data-Driven Discovery and a recipient of the National Science Foundation CAREER "Young Investigators" Award. He is a proponent of open and reproducible science and serves on the the Software Carpentry Advisory Council, the Data Carpentry steering committee, the Impactstory board of directors, and the editorial boards of PLOS ONE and PeerJ.
