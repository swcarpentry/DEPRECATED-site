--- layout: blog author: Greg Wilson title: Washington Went Well date: 2013-03-01 time: 11:00:00 category: [boot-camp, venues/university-of-washington] ---

Last week, we put on the largest Software Carpentry boot camp ever at the University of Washington: three rooms, six instructors, ten helpers, and 93 students spent two days together learning about Python, Git, the Unix shell, and a bunch of other useful things. Many thanks to Prof. Bill Howe for making it all possible—we look forward to coming back soon.
