Highlights

  • The next online lab meeting will take place on 1 April at 10:00 and 19:00 Eastern time. Remember to sign up.

Resources

  • Jenny Bryan contributed a Gapminder data package now available through CRAN which might be helpful in setting up for Software Carpentry workshops.
  • Data Carpentry developed new material for teaching dplyr using their ecological dataset.

Opportunities

  • Students can apply to participate in our Google Summer of Code projects until 19:00 UTC on 27 March 2015. We have several exciting projects available.
  • Insight launched a new seven week fellows program in Health Data Science.

Details

  • Uwe Hilgert described their recent experiences at an iPlant workshop. They built a curriculum covering everything from data cleaning to generating publishable analysis and diagrams.
  • Four workshop were run last week: Utah State University, Saint Francis Xavier University, University of Arkansas, and University of Waterloo.
  • Several workshops have been confirmed for the next few months. Upcoming workshops can be viewed on our website.
    • March: Harvard School of Public Health, University of Oklahoma Libraries
    • April: University of Washinghton - Seattle, Imperial College London, Northwestern University, University of California - Santa Barbara, University of Texas at Arlington, Washington State University, Simon Fraser University
    • May: Western University, University of Melbourne
    • July: The University of Queensland, The University of Queensland - AMOS sciences
