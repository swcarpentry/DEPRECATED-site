We had a great birds of a feather session at the SciPy conference last week and decided to create a mailing list specifically for instructors who are interested in using the Jupyter Notebook for teaching. The aim of the mailing list is to provide a place for instructors to share materials, strategies, advice, etc. on teaching with the notebook and the logistics that are involved with that. If you're interested in joining the mailing list as well, you can add yourself at https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/jupyter-education

We look forward to chatting with you there.
