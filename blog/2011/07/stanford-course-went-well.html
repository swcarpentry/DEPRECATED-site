Prof. Risa Wechsler, along with Alex Ji and Zahan Malkani, recently ran a short course at Stanford based in part on Software Carpentry called Physics 91S1: Practical Computing for Scientists. The course notes and handouts are available online, and the open source bits of code are on GitHub. We hope you find them useful...
