The Software Carpentry Foundation's membership is made up of:

  1. Every qualified instructor who has taught at least twice in the past two calendar years.

  2. Anyone who has done 30 days or more work for the SCF in the past calendar year.

  3. Anyone who has, in the opinion of the Steering Committee, made a significant contribution in the past year.

Members elect the Steering Committee, and may also seek election to it. This list was last updated on December 3, 2015; please report any errors.
